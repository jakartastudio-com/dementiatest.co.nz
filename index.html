Dementia Tests Screening Application

DementiaTest app is a screening application, which is solely for research purposes and not for commercial use. This will fulfills 6-CIT and SCIDS tests terms of use. The app contains Dementia screening tests to help you or your caregiver make an assessment of your mental health. You will find a useful questionnaire to gauge your mental health. These tests are not diagnostic tools rather they are behavioural tests that just pinpoint to Dementia disorder. The app can be freely downloaded and distributed but cannot be used for financial gain. This app was developed by Dr Fadi Fayez Thabtah & Ella Mampusti with a research support team including  Raymund Mark, Mohit Nandu and Omar Ibrahim. This app is used to detect Dementia symptoms in its early stages. A set of questions related to functional and cognitive will be provided to determine the dementia symptoms. In the end, the score that will be a pinpoint to any dementia traits will be shown. This app is suitable for patients and caregivers alike.
